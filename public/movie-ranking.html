Michael's Movie List

My Top 3 Movies of all time!

The Shawshank Redemption

I like The Shawshank Redemption because it beautifully portrays hope, friendship, and perseverance in the face of immense adversity, reminding us that resilience can lead to freedom.

Inception

I like Inception because it masterfully blends complex storytelling with stunning visuals, exploring the power of the mind and the blurred lines between reality and dreams.

Django Unchained

II like Django Unchained for its bold storytelling, unforgettable characters, and its unique blend of action, humor, and powerful themes of justice and revenge.
